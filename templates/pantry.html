How it works

  • Ingredients marked as staples will show as "Check Pantry" reminders in your grocery list
  • Mark items as "LOW" when you're running out - they'll appear in your grocery list
  • Click "LOW" again to mark as restocked
  • Click "#" next to any item to switch to quantity tracking mode
  • In quantity mode, use +/- to track how many you have. When recipes use up your supply and it hits 0, the item auto-adds to your grocery list
