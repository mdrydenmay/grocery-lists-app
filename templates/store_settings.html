How It Works

  • Ingredients are automatically assigned to stores based on built-in patterns (e.g. produce goes to Trader Joe's, salmon goes to Whole Foods)
  • On the Grocery List page, use the dropdown next to any item to change its store
  • Your choice is saved as an override and will apply to future grocery lists
  • Remove an override here to revert to auto-detection
  • Use the pencil button on the Grocery List to correct ingredient names that were parsed incorrectly
